Proatmeal

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup organic rolled oats
  • 1.33 cup steaming water
  • 1 tsp saigon cinnamon
  • 20-30 grams of deisred protein powder

Steps to follow:

  1. Start hot-pot or steam water
  2. Add 1/2 cup oats in large bowl
  3. Add saigon cinnamon in bowl
  4. Add steaming water, stir until mixed, let sit for 10 minutes
  5. Add 20-30g of protein from powder of choice
  6. Add water if alteration of consistency is desired
