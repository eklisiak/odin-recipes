Protein Shake


Ingredients:

  • 16oz water
  • 60-70g favorite protein powder
  • 1tsp Saigon cinnamon
  • (optional) Frozen wild berries
  • (optional) Frozen banana

Steps to follow:

  1. Fill shaker cup to 16oz, 12oz if you want the shake thick
  2. Add 60-70g of protein
  3. Add 1tps of Saigon cinnamon
  4. (If blending) Add 1 cup of frozen mixed berries and 1 banana
